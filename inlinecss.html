Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato (18BCE0928) -YOGESWARI SAHU

Inline CSS

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices Cooked Bacon
  • 5 Tablespoons soft Butter, Divided (or to taste)
  • 4 Slices bread (white or whole wheat)
  • 4 Slices Monterey Jack pepper cheese (or use American cheese)
  • 4 Slices Tomatoes

Instructions

1) Spread about 1 tablespoon butter into the bottom of a large skillet (or just leave some fat in the pan from the bacon).
2) Spread 1 tablespoon (can use more) onto one side of each slice of bread.
3) Place the 4 slices of bread butter-side down onto a large skillet or flat grill.
4) Top with 1 slice of cheese, 2 slices of tomato and 2 slices cooked bacon, then 1 more slice of cheese.
5) Cover with a slice of bread (butter-side facing outside).
6) Fry over medium turning once with a spatula until golden brown on both sides.
